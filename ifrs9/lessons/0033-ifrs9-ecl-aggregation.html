IFRS9 ECL Aggregation

Goal: calculate and reconcile segment-level ECL in Snowflake.

The calculation can be simple; the control totals must be strict.

Core Example

select
  IFRS9_STAGE,
  PRODUCT_CODE,
  sum(EAD) as TOTAL_EAD,
  sum(EAD * PD * LGD) as TOTAL_ECL
from IFRS9_STAGE_BASE
group by IFRS9_STAGE, PRODUCT_CODE;

Hands-On Exercise

  1. Add sample PD and LGD columns.
  2. Calculate ECL at account level.
  3. Reconcile account totals to segment totals.
