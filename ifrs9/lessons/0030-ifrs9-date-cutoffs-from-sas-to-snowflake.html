IFRS9 Date Cutoffs From SAS To Snowflake

Goal: replace SAS date literals and reporting-period filters with explicit Snowflake dates using the synthetic IFRS9 dataset.

IFRS9 runs are cutoff-sensitive. Make every reporting date visible, typed, and reconcilable.

Core Example

-- SAS: where as_of_dt = '31MAY2026'd
where dateadd(day, AS_OF_DT, date '1960-01-01') = date '2026-05-31'

Hands-On Exercise

  1. Open sas_programs/ifrs9_ecl_batch.sas from the dataset.
  2. Find &as_of_dt and every _DT column used by the loan-account flow.
  3. Count rows by converted AS_OF_DT before and after the translation.
