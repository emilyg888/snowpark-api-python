Building Facts and Bridges in dbt

Goal: build the fact table and extras bridge while preserving the intended customer x vehicle grain.

Your fact grain is deliberate: one row per source sale, customer element, and vehicle element combination.

Fact Key

md5(
  base.src::string || '|' ||
  customer.customer_index::string || '|' ||
  vehicle.vehicle_index::string
) as sale_key

The key includes both indexes because one source sale can create multiple fact rows.

Bridge Grain

one row = sale_key + extra

The bridge model flattens vehicle.extras after the customer and vehicle grain has already been established.
