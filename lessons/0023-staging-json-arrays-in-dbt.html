Staging JSON Arrays in dbt

Goal: flatten customer and vehicle arrays once in staging models, then reuse them in marts.

Staging models are where raw shape becomes reliable columns. For CAR_SALES, that means extracting top-level fields and flattening arrays.

The Three Staging Models

stg_car_sales_base
One row per raw JSON document.
stg_car_sales_customers
One row per sale and customer index.
stg_car_sales_vehicles
One row per sale and vehicle index.

The Pattern

from {{ ref('stg_car_sales_base') }} base,
  lateral flatten(input => base.src:vehicle) vehicle

Keep the array index. It becomes part of the fact grain when you intentionally combine customers and vehicles.
