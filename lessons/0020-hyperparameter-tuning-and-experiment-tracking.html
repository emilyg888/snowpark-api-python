Hyperparameter Tuning and Experiment Tracking

Goal: compare many model configurations and record the parameters and metrics that explain the result.

The lab uses Snowflake ML tuning to run multiple XGBoost trials, then logs trial parameters and metrics to experiment tracking.

The Mental Model

Search space
The allowed ranges for max_depth, learning_rate, and n_estimators.
Trial
One model training run with one generated hyperparameter set.
Metric
The score used to choose the best trial, such as F1_Test.
Experiment
The record of parameters and metrics across runs.
