Deploying the dbt Cloud Flow

Goal: turn the car-sales dbt models into a repeatable dbt Cloud job.

In dbt Cloud, development proves the DAG works. A deployment job makes it repeatable with run history, logs, and artifacts.

Recommended Job

Commands:
