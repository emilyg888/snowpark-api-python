Datasets, Preprocessing, and Baseline Training

Goal: generate a training dataset from feature views, encode categoricals, split it, and train a baseline model.

The lab uses Feature Store to assemble the dataset, Snowpark ML preprocessing for one-hot encoding, and pandas only at the final scikit-learn/XGBoost training boundary.

The Pipeline

Dataset
fs.generate_dataset(...) joins a spine to feature views.
Preprocess
OneHotEncoder turns string columns into numeric model inputs.
Split
random_split creates train and test Snowpark DataFrames.
Train
to_pandas() crosses into local XGBoost training.
